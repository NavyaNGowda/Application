Tadiandamol Trek

  • Tadiandamol trek is one of the most beautiful, though less known, treks in Karnataka. The highest peak in Coorg/Kodagu district Tadiandimol trek takes you to an elevation of 5740 ft.

The best view comes after the hardest climb

Highlights

  • Distance from Bangalore – 250 Kms by road.
  • Difficulty Level – Easy to Moderate
  • Trek distance – 10-11 Kms (two way)
  • Time to summit – 5 hours (one way).
  • Food: Carry packed lunch
  • Permission: Required

The Path which leads us to Peak

The trek starts from Nalknad Palace in Kakkabe, about 45km from Madikeri. You can go up the road in vehicle till the last home stay from where a concrete road begins which gradually narrows into the wild
